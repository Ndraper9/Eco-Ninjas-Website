NHS Case Study · Somerset Foundation Trust

Real Results from Musgrove Park Hospital

Somerset NHS Foundation Trust implemented Eco Ninjas badge hats across their theatre departments in Spring 2024. The transformation was immediate and measurable — with clinical staff and patients reporting overwhelmingly positive feedback within the first month.

The badge identification system revolutionised team communication, particularly during critical surgical moments where knowing exact roles is the difference between smooth operations and avoidable delays.

  • 90,000 disposable caps saved from incineration in year one
  • Staff recognition rate increased from 41.1% to 95.9%
  • 48% cost savings achieved across the three-year period
  • Enhanced WHO Surgical Safety Checklist compliance
